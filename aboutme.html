Lily Liu 刘津溢

Lily is a Junior at Babson College in Wellesley Hills, MA. She has been learning the Guzheng since she was eight years old. She has received the highest level of music degree from the China Conservatory of Music in 2016, and has been competing in multiple local and worldwide competitions since 2012. She has placed top three in competitions such as San Francisco Youth Talent, Silicon Valley Music Competition, and International Music Competitions. To share her performances with others, she recently started her own Facebook page and YouTube channel with more than 10,000 subscribers totally. Her first music video, a cover of "See You Again", has received over 200 million views so far. She not only have passion in the Chinese Zither, she has also been participating in Pageants. In 2017, she won the title of 2017 Miss America Fashion Travel, then later gaining recognition for Best Talent Award at the Miss World Fashion Beauty Pageant. In 2016, she won First Runner Up (Second Place) in US region of Miss World Fashion Beauty Pageant ending her pageant adventures. She says that on her path to being successful in playing the Chinese Zither, it has taught her that failure is the best way to learn in addition to hours of practice. She is a high performing student in her school, receiving full four year Diversity Leadership Scholarship.
